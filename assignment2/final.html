NBA Analysis in United States

Author: Guohua Wu

The NBA has fans all over the world. In order to know more about NBA players and teams, This report will mainly discuss NBA teams and players that get excellent results.Firstly, it will introduce the locations of NBA teams in United States. Meanwhile, it also shows the games are won and losed for NBA teams. To further explore NBA teams the report contains top five champtionship team to show the avergae points during thier championship sessions. Players are also important to teams, thus, there are top5 MVP active players with four different variables to analysis their performance.

Locations of NBA teams

This map shows the locations of NBA teams in the United States. Most teams are on the coast or along rivers. Becuase it needs to attract more audiences to watch games with convenient trafffics. Meanwhile, the economic development for coastal cities is faster than inland cities, whihch can support ecxpense for teams. There are four NBA teams ( Los Angeles Lakers, Sacramento Kings, Golden State Warriors and Los Angeles Clippers ) in California state, meanwhile, Texas state also have three NBA teams (Dallas Mavericks, San Antonio Spurs, Houston Rockets). Most of states have two or one NBA teams.

Win games

The bubbler plot shows the win games, lose games and total games per NBA team. There are six divisions (Pacific division, Central division, Atlantic division, Southeast division, Southwest division and Northwest Division ). The most outstanding teams are Los Angeles Lakers and Boston Celtics, Los Angeles Lakers has 3460-win games and 59.2% win/lose rate, which is higher than other teams. Boston Celtics has 3513-win games and 59.0% win /lose rate follow the Los Angeles Lakers.

Average Point per game

This idiom shows the top five championship teams (Boston Celtics, Los Angeles Lakers, Golden State Warriors, Chicago Bulls and San Antonio Suprs ). Boston Celtics and Los Angeles Lakers won 17 champs; Golden State Warriors won 7 champs followed the two teams. The average point per game for Boston Celtics over 100 during the champ sessions, which played very steadily. While the average point per game for Los Angeles Lakers is waved between 88 to 122. Overall, those top five championship teams’ average point per game are floated between 90 to 128.

Top 5 MVP

Those area charts choose four variables to analysis top five MVP active players. The four variables include field goal percentage, total rebounds per game, point per game and assists per game. In addition, LeBron James got four MVP, Nikola Jokic, Stephen Curry and Giannis Antetokounmpo got 2 MVP and James Harden got 1 MVP. It is clearly seen that the player’s total scores were stable and fluctuated along similar curve. For the total rebounds per game , Nikola shows an outstanding performance which is higher than other four players. Meanwhile, the point of rebounds for Giannis is gradually increase during recent years. James Harden and LeBron James perform well for assistants between 2016 and 2019. Stephen Curry and James Harden have excellent points from 2015 to 2018. Those four variables show the different strengths and weaknesses of each player. Additionally, each players play different important roles in their teams.
