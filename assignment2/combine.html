NBA Location Map

This map shows the locations of NBA teams in the United States. There are four NBA teams ( Los Angeles Lakers, Sacramento Kings, Golden State Warriors and Los Angeles Clippers ) in California state, meanwhile, Texas state also have three NBA teams (Dallas Mavericks, San Antonio Spurs, Houston Rockets). Most of states have two or one NBA teams.

Win games per NBA Team

The bubbler plot shows the win games, lose games and total games per NBA team. There are six divisions (Pacific division, Central division, Atlantic division, Southeast division, Southwest division and Northwest Division ). The most outstanding teams are Los Angeles Lakers and Boston Celtics, Los Angeles Lakers has 3460-win games and 59.2% win/lose rate, which is higher than other teams. Boston Celtics has 3513-win games and 59.0% win /lose rate follow the Los Angeles Lakers.
